About

Texts like the Universal Declaration of Human Rights derive part of their power from their conciseness. This project tries to answer the question: does defining the words in these texts make them clearer or less clear? Can you kill a text by over-defining it? And how hard is it to keep this expanded text apart from an expanded version of a much more mundane text?
